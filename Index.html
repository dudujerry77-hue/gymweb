FAQ

Questions Answered

Is there a joining fee or contract?

No joining fee and no lock-in contracts. All plans are month-to-month and can be cancelled at any time with 30 days' notice. We believe we earn your membership every single month.

What are the gym opening hours?

Standard access is 5:30 AM – 11:00 PM Monday to Friday, and 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM on weekends. APEX members enjoy 24/7 keycard access to the main floor year-round.

Can I try the gym before joining?

Absolutely. Every new member gets a 7-day free trial — full access, no credit card required. Just book a walkthrough session with one of our coaches and we'll get you started.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

Yes — 20% student discount with valid ID, and corporate packages for teams of 5 or more. We also run referral rewards where both you and a friend get a free month when they join.

What equipment does the gym have?

10,000 sq ft of premium kit: 20+ power racks, full cable station suite, cardio deck, dedicated functional fitness zone, boxing ring, and a full recovery suite with sauna, ice bath, and massage guns.

How do I book a class or PT session?

Through the FORGE app or the member portal on our website. Classes can be booked up to 7 days in advance. PT sessions are booked directly with your coach. APEX and FORGE members get priority booking 24 hours earlier.
